Schedule

The first three days combine guest lectures, workshops and site visits.

The rest of the masterclass is dedicated to participant-led projects: students will select one of four research topics introduced by the teaching staff, design a collective research protocol, and work in small groups.

On the last afternoon, each group will present the output of their projects to discuss their findings and reflections with the entire cohort.

Notes

  • (*) Each day will conclude on a Debrief & Discussion (D&D), during which we expect participants to reflect on the day’s contents, using their notes to confront their impressions and analysis.

  • Unless specified, all guest lectures and workshops will take place on the UCT campus in the Centlivres Building in the south end of the upper campus.

  • Unless specified, every Debrief & Discussion will take place at the Lenox Guest House. Provisions have been taken to allow for UCT students to enter the premises upon presentation of their student cards.

  • Friday 7th is a public holiday in South Africa: the masterclass will nonetheless takes place on that day. The following Sunday and Monday will be considered as days off.
