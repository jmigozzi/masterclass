Overview

Welcome to the International Masterclass on “Digital Technologies in the African city”!

This event is co-organised by the Department of Geography from the Ecole normale supérieure, the African Centre for Cities at the University of Cape Town, and the IFRA-Nigeria, with the financial support of the Translitterae program.

The masterclass will gather 20 graduate students enrolled in Nigeria, France and South Africa for 8 days of conferences, workshops and fieldwork. We will question and research how digital technologies, from data centres to smartphone apps, reshape urban economies and daily livelihoods, within and beyond the context of Cape Town, one of Africa’s major tech hubs.

The masterclass will take place on the UCT campus from April 3rd to April 12th 2023.
